Our Story

Green Thumb Gardens, LLC was founded in 1985 by James Swenson as a landscaping company.

Years later, in 2021, he expanded the company and opened the greenhouse we operate today.

Over the years, we've cultivated a reputation for dependability, blending expert techniques with a commitment to nurturing healthy, vibrant plants."

Our Core Values

  • Excellence: We strive for perfection in every project, from the smallest garden bed to the largest landscape installation.
  • Sustainability: We are committed to eco-conscious practices, promoting biodiversity, water conservation, and the use of native plants.
  • Integrity: We operate with honesty, transparency, and respect.
  • Passion: Our love for gardening and the outdoors fuels our dedication to our craft.
